About Us

The Heritage Roadmap Foundation is dedicated to improving human health and wellness through two collaborative programs. Our first program focuses on nutrition and sustainability as a means to combat poor health and hunger. Our second program is focused on providing digital health products and services to individuals with chronic non-communicable diseases, as well as exploring the field of eco-health. Our mission is to make a positive impact on people's lives through our innovative and effective health care solutions.

Our Values Preposition

Our values prepositions are fundamentally centered on impartiality, humanity, neutrality, and accountability. These values guide Heritage Roadmap Foundation in making ethical and effective decisions that prioritize the well-being of affected communities, protect the dignity of those in need, and ensure that aid is delivered in a manner that is transparent, accountable, and respects the rights of beneficiaries.

Our Agile Methodology

Overall, a coordinated and integrated approach to humanitarian interventions and mitigation efforts can help to ensure that communities are better prepared to withstand and recover from crisis events and those humanitarian interventions are more effective in addressing the needs of affected populations.

Creating operational wings within a Non-Profit Organization (NPO) can have several benefits and purposes, including:

  • Specialization : By dividing the organization into different operational units, each unit can focus on a specific area of intervention and become specialized in that area. This allows the NPO to maximize its impact and efficiency in delivering its services.
  • Improved accountability : Having separate operational units also means that each unit can be held accountable for its own performance and activities. This helps to ensure that resources are being used effectively and that the NPO is making progress towards its goals.
  • Better management : Dividing the organization into operational units can also help to streamline decision-making and improve management of the NPO. This can lead to more effective and efficient use of resources, and a greater ability to respond to challenges and opportunities

Overall, creating operational wings within an NPO and further dividing those units into separate sub-bank accounts can help to improve the efficiency, accountability, and transparency of the organization. These steps can help the NPO to maximize its impact and achieve its goals more effectively.
